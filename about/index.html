Our Vision

As students, we think education technology sucks. It's fragmented, uses super old legacy technology, and seems like it's stuck in 2005. We decided that we'd like to solve these problems:

  • Fragmentation — Students are told to go to 2 or 3 different services to get information about different stuff.
  • Unreliability — Oftentimes, the services that students use to e.g. view their grades are down, they crash, or other issues that make them unreliable.
  • Sub-par Design — Lots of services have very poor design that's not very pleasing to the eye.
We proposed a solution: unify everything into one place. So we made Academus. While developing the app, we kept these goals in mind:
  • Ease of Use — Keep the UI simple. Show relevant information front and center.
  • Elegant Design — We want students to enjoy using our app, so we need to make it "wow" them.
  • Uniqueness — We want to stand out from the crowd. We're not simply another app that lets you check your grades; we're more than that.
