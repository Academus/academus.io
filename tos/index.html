Appropriate Use

With Academus, you are able to communicate with other students, and we trust that you will create a safe environment for everyone to discuss in. However, we realize that bad people exist in the world, so we have put in place several systems to safeguard the rest of the Academus community from these people.

We have written community guidelines to follow in Academus Chat or wherever you may interact with the rest of the Academus community, and we hope that you'll follow them. If you find someone drifting away from these guidelines, there's a system in place that lets you report other users' messages. Tap on a message (long-tap on iOS) and tap the "Report" button to flag a message as potentially violating our guidelines

If we find that one of your messages is in violation of the Academus community guidelines, we may delete your message or restrict you from participating in Academus Chat entirely. We genuinely hope that it doesn't come to this, but we will take appropriate action if need be.
