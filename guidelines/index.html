Community Guidelines

The foundation for a safe community

Why these guidelines exist

We wrote these guidelines to help facilitate a safer Academus community where everyone feels welcome. You should follow these guidelines when participating in Academus Chat.

Rules you should follow

When participating in Academus communities (e.g. Chat), please follow these guidelines:

  • Be excellent to each other
  • Keep things mostly school appropriate
    • As teenagers ourselves, we understand that we're not always in line with what schools define as "appropriate". For example, stuff like mild cussing and non-offensive memes are okay.
  • Don't use racial slurs or other discriminatory language
  • Don't personally attack any other students
  • Don't do anything that you might regret in the future
  • Don't attempt to impersonate Academus staff
    • It would be silly to, since Academus staff members have a green badge next to their name that says "staff". If they don't have this badge, then they aren't staff.

If you follow these general guidelines, you'll be okay! We hope that these guidelines help maintain a safe and welcoming environment in the Academus community.

Reporting inappropriate behavior

If someone should violate these guidelines, we have systems in place to report bad behavior from other users.

Simply tap a user's message, then "Report". We'll receive your report and look into it ASAP. If we find that the user you reported has violated these guidelines, we will delete their message, ban them, or both based on the severity of the violation.
