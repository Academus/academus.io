Welcome to the 2018-2019 school year!

We know it might be hard coming back for some of you, while others couldn't be happier for the start of a new school year. No matter who you are, we have a plethora of new features we will be rolling out this semester.

Academus Chat

Possibly one of our biggest features, Academus Chat lets you talk to your classmates without ever having to set up a group chat manually. Group chats for each of your classes are automatically made and students join automatically when they join Academus.

Academus Scholar

Over the summer we realized something: we have no money! Our hosting isn't free, so we had to figure out a way to break even. We came up with Academus Scholar: a premium version of Academus for those wanting to get the most out of the app. Academus will still be free, this is simply an upgrade.
