Welcome to the 2018-2019 school year!

We know coming back might be hard for some of you, while others couldn't be happier for the start of a new school year. No matter who you are, we have a plethora of new features we will be rolling out this semester that we think you'll enjoy.

Academus Chat

Possibly one of our biggest features, Academus Chat lets you talk to your classmates without ever having to set up a group chat manually. Group chats for each of your classes are automatically made and students join automatically when they join Academus.

Academus Scholar

We came up with Academus Scholar: a premium version of Academus for those wanting to get the most out of the app. Academus will still be free, this is simply an upgrade. Grade History

Those of you on Android have already seen this feature, but now it's coming to iOS! With Grade History, you can see how your grade changed over time with a handy chart.
