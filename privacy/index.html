Privacy Policy

What data we collect and how we protect it

What data does Academus collect?

When you sign up for an account, Academus will collect information including, but not limited to:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • A password for your account, which is later hashed and salted in our database

When you add an integration to your Academus account, the specific information varies. For StudentVUE integrations, Academus will collect:

  • Your StudentVUE username
  • Your StudentVUE password, which is later tokenized and salted in our database

For PowerSchool integrations, Academus will collect:

  • Your district code
  • Your PowerSchool username
  • Your PowerSchool password, which is later tokenized and salted in our database

Other data that is collected can include, but is not limited to:

  • Your legal name (provided by SIS integrations)
  • Your physical address
  • Your phone number
  • Other personal information provided by integrations

We never store any unnecessary info like your physical address in our database, but we'd like to be transparent about every bit of data that goes through our infrastructure.

How does Academus use your data?

Our bottom line is this: your data is yours, you have control over it, and only you will ever see it. Due to the sensitivity of data we are storing, your data will never be for sale, no matter what the price.

We will never share any personally-identifiable information about you publicly. However, we may use anonymized data to bring useful statistics to other students such as, but not limited to:

  • Average grade for a course
  • How many students take a course
  • Your performance compared to other students

You can, of course, opt out of data analytics through the Privacy and Security section in Settings. If you do, however, you will not be able to view these statistics from within the app.

We take extra care to ensure that grade data is never able to be traced back to a single person. All data is anonymized and aggregated before being displayed anywhere.

How does Academus protect data?

We know that student information systems can hold sensitive information about a user, and this is why we've taken several steps to ensure that your data is safe and secure. We use industry-standard SSL encryption in all communications between the app and server, and tokenize third-party usernames and passwords whenever possible.

Our infrastructure is hosted on Azure, with each server having isolated Dockerized containers that only expose the resources necessary (such as the API server) for the app to work. Every server we own is protected by a private key and has the latest security updates.
